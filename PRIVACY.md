# Privacy Policy for Construction Field App

**Last Updated:** February 2025

## Introduction

Construction Field ("we", "our", "us") operates the Construction Field mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for admin accounts)
- Phone number (for crew member authentication via SMS)
- Full name and job title
- Company/organization information
- User profile preferences

### Usage Data
- Task creation and completion history
- Form submissions and responses
- Inspection records and status
- Photos uploaded to job sites and associated metadata
- Location data (when using location-based features)
- Crew workload and time tracking data

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version number
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance metrics and diagnostic data
- Biometric authentication status (Face ID/Touch ID enablement)

### Authentication Data
- PIN codes (hashed and encrypted, never stored in plain text)
- Authentication tokens (temporary, auto-refreshing)
- Session information

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
- Authenticate users securely with PIN, phone, email, and biometric authentication
- Enable seamless collaboration between crew members and administrators
- Store and retrieve task, inspection, and form submission records
- Improve app performance, stability, and user experience
- Send important notifications about app updates, critical features, and service changes
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
- Monitor for security threats and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- All data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
- Data is automatically synced to secure cloud servers
- Offline data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage

### Security Measures
- All data transmissions use HTTPS with TLS encryption
- User passwords are never stored locally or on servers (Firebase handles authentication)
- PIN codes are hashed using bcrypt and stored securely
- Sensitive data (tokens, biometric data) is encrypted at rest
- Firebase Security Rules restrict access to authorized users only
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### Data Retention
- Active project data is retained for the duration of your subscription
- Archived projects and completed tasks can be deleted by admins
- Backup data is retained according to Firebase's backup policy
- Account deletion removes all associated personal data (subject to legal holds)

## Data Sharing

### What We Do NOT Do
We do NOT sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal data to third parties.

### When We Share Data
We share data only in the following circumstances:

1. **Within Your Organization**: Authorized crew members and administrators can access task and inspection data relevant to their roles
2. **Service Providers**:
   - Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) - data hosting and authentication
   - Sentry - error tracking and crash reporting (error context only, no sensitive data)
3. **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government request
4. **Business Transfers**: In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- **Right to Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Right to Export**: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Disable non-essential data collection and analytics
- **Right to Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:
- Email: support@yourcompany.com
- Website: https://yourcompany.com/contact

We will respond to requests within 30 days.

## Data Protection Compliance

This App complies with applicable data protection regulations:
- GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act)
- Other regional privacy laws

## Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will immediately delete such information and terminate their account.

## Biometric Data

If you enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint), that biometric data:
- Is never stored on our servers
- Is processed only by your device's secure enclave/trusted execution environment
- Is used only to unlock your session with the App
- Can be disabled at any time in your device's settings

## Third-Party Services

This App uses the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Authentication**: Phone and email sign-in
- **Firebase Firestore**: Data storage and real-time synchronization
- **Firebase Storage**: Photo and document storage
- **Sentry**: Error and crash reporting
- **Expo Services**: App development and deployment (during development only)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them at:
- Firebase: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Sentry: https://sentry.io/privacy/
- Expo: https://expo.dev/privacy

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:
- Notify you via in-app notification
- Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document
- Request your consent to any material changes (if required by law)

Your continued use of the App following notification of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify affected users within 72 hours
- Provide information about the nature of the breach
- Offer credit monitoring or other protective measures (if applicable)
- Cooperate with law enforcement and regulatory agencies
